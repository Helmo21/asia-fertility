# Deputy Prime Minister Le Tien Chau to attend Nikkei Forum Future of Asia in Tokyo

FUTURE OF ASIA is an international gathering where political, economic, and academic leaders from the Asia-Pacific region offer their opinions frankly and freely on regional issues and the role of Asia in the world. 
Held annually by Nikkei since 1995, it is considered to be one of the most important global forums in Asia.
Theme of 31st FUTURE of ASIA is "Working together for a more resilient and prosperous Asia." The world has entered a volatile period of transition. The post-war global order faces multiple challenges, and Asia must find its own answers to navigate through rough waters.
The 31st Future of Asia will discuss and offer valuable ideas on how we can work closely together for a much more resilient and prosperous Asia./.
