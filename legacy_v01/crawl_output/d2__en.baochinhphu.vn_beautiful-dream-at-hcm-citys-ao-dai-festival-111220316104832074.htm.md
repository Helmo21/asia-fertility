# Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival 
##  VGP – Designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam's glittering 'Dream' collection has made strong impression at the ongoing HCM City's Ao Dai Festival. 
Author [Thanh Ha | ](javascript:; "Thanh Ha")
March 16, 2022 10:48 AM GMT+7 
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 1.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh1-16474019961411880821820.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh1-16474019961411880821820.jpg "The ao dai collection entitled Dream by designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam is one of the highlights at the HCM City's Ao Dai Festival which will last until April 15. Photos of VGP")
The ao dai collection entitled Dream by designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam is one of the highlights at the HCM City's Ao Dai Festival which will last until April 15. Photos of VGP
The eighth fair features numerous designs of traditional long dress of national famous designers and the participation of hundreds of models and celebrities.  

The event, which will last until April 15, aims to introduce the beauty and convenience of ao dai, making it familiar costumes of people in contribution to preserving the nation's cultural identity and promoting the image of the country and Vietnamese people.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 2.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh2-1647401996146124228117.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh2-1647401996146124228117.jpg "Ao dai traditional dresses are designed following an inspiration from Bat Trang ceramic quintessence.")
Ao dai traditional dresses are designed following an inspiration from Bat Trang ceramic quintessence.
The festival has a great significance for the city that has gradually returned to the new normal state while the economy is recovering. It affirms the city government's determination to recover and develop the economy after the COVID-19 pandemic, introducing to local and international visitors images of a modern and vivid city, towards the building of an exciting tourism city in Asia.
**Introducing ao dai to the world**
Designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam's 'Dream' was one of 15 collections, which were divided into four major themes of Origin, Sublimation, Integration and My Beloved City, introduced in the festival's opening ceremony.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 3.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh3-16474019961511404215095.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh3-16474019961511404215095.jpg "Hundreds of local artisans from craft villages around Ha Noi have taken part in making the ao dai.")
Hundreds of local artisans from craft villages around Ha Noi have taken part in making the ao dai.
Inspired of the unique characteristics of Bat Trang ceramics, Nam drew beautiful and colourful pictures on his designs.
With the main tones of yellow, blue, orange, his "Dream" Ao Dai Collection creates a fresh and vibrant image for the festive outfits for Spring-Summer.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 4.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh4-1647401996155439851683.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh4-1647401996155439851683.jpg "Colourful ao dai dresses make up fresh and vibrant Spring-Summer collection.")
Colourful ao dai dresses make up fresh and vibrant Spring-Summer collection.
Typically, all of the designs are made by artisans from renowned traditional craft villages in Thuong Tin, Chuong My and My Duc districts in Ha Noi. 
Artisans have worked from six months to a year to complete ao dai designs that are not just garment products but works of art.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 5.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh5-1647401996224406815938.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh5-1647401996224406815938.jpg "Artisan spent up to a year to produce state-of-art ao dais.")
Artisan spent up to a year to produce state-of-art ao dais.
**Outstanding designed ao dai**
Do Trinh Hoai Nam has been considered one of leading ao dai designers in Viet Nam with his pioneering position in creating the long dress trends. 
He, on the other hand, also has made his own typical designs that are mixed with tradition and in tune with world fashion trends.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 6.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh6-16474019964211719331970.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh6-16474019964211719331970.jpg "Adding ceramic patterns into ao dai, designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam has shown off his amazing creativity in designing.")
Adding ceramic patterns into ao dai, designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam has shown off his amazing creativity in designing.
Ao dai designs in the collection 'Dream' are made of silk and velvet decorated with handcrafted velvet beads to make up 'gem paintings' on the dresses. Ceramic patterns in combination with his top-notch hand embroidery techniques have made Do Trinh Hoai Nam's ao dai glittering and shining. 
The COVID-19 pandemic has forced many culture and lifestyle event delayed or canceled in almost two years.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 7.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh7-16474019965662115996454.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh7-16474019965662115996454.jpg "Do Trinh Hoai Nam's outstanding technique plays key roles in keeping ao dai's fashionable and charming form and sexy and elegant appearance.")
Do Trinh Hoai Nam's outstanding technique plays key roles in keeping ao dai's fashionable and charming form and sexy and elegant appearance.
However, designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam did not rest. It was time that he focused totally on designing.
His colourful and joyful ao dai dresses convey positive messages about a peaceful, happy and healthy life and hope of a win over the pandemic.
**Ao dai -- Viet Nam's soul**
It is said that wherever Vietnamese people are there will have ao dai which represents Vietnamese soul and pride.
[![Beautiful 'Dream' at HCM City's Ao Dai Festival   - Ảnh 8.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh8-16474019967261285562238.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/3/16/anh8-16474019967261285562238.jpg "The eighth ao dai festival themed 'I Love Vietnamese Ao Dai' is an affirmation that HCM City has come back strongly and fast comeback and become a safe area against the COVID-19 epidemic.")
The eighth ao dai festival themed 'I Love Vietnamese Ao Dai' is an affirmation that HCM City has come back strongly and fast comeback and become a safe area against the COVID-19 epidemic.
The traditional dress has also made up name of designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam who has been familiar face in local and international catwalk with his typical and unique ao dai collections.
Last November, designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam introduced a big collection of ao dai in the US. Earlier, he also took part in many fashion weeks in Paris and New York where ao dai were showed off by international models and received remarkable feedbacks.  

As president of the Viet Nam Ao Dai Club, designer Do Trinh Hoai Nam understood that his mission is not only to preserve the national beauty symbol abut also to bring ao dai to the world./.
