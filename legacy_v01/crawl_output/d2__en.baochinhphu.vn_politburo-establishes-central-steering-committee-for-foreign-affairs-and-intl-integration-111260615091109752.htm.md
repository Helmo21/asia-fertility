# Politburo establishes Central Steering Committee for Foreign Affairs and Int'l Integration
##  VGP – The Politburo has decided to establish the Central Steering Committee for Foreign Affairs and International Integration, led by General Secretary and President To Lam. 
[Huong Giang](javascript:; "Huong Giang")
June 15, 2026 9:10 AM GMT+7

Politburo member, Prime Minister Le Minh Hung will serve as Permanent Deputy Head of the Central Steering Committee.
Politburo member, Chairman of the National Assembly Tran Thanh Man and Politburo member, Standing member of the Party Central Committee's Secretariat will serve as Deputy Heads of the Central Steering Committee.
Politburo member, Foreign Minister Le Hoai Trung will serve as permanent member of the Central Steering Committee.
Members of the Central Steering Committee include: Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, President of the Viet Nam Fatherland Front Central Committee Bui Thi Minh Hoai; Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Central Organization Commission Nguyen Duy Ngoc; Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Central Commission for Education, Communication and Mass Mobilization Trinh Van Quyet; Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Central Commission for Internal Affairs Le Minh Tri; Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Central Inspection Commission Nguyen Sy Thanh; Politburo member, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Central Policy and Strategy Commission Nguyen Thanh Nghi; Politburo member, Standing Deputy Prime Minister Pham Gia Tuc; Politburo member, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Phan Van Giang; Politburo member, Minister of Public Security Luong Tam Quang; Politburo member, Director of the Ho Chi Minh National Academy of Politics Doan Minh Huan; Politburo member, Secretary of Ha Noi Municipal Party Committee Tran Duc Thang; and Politburo member, Secretary of Ho Chi Minh City Municipal Party Committee Tran Luu Quang.
Other members of the Central Steering Committee include: Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Thi Thanh Tra; Chairman of the National Assembly's Committee for National Defense, Security and Foreign Affairs; Chairman of the Party Central Committee's Office Nguyen Hai Ninh; Minister of Finance Ngo Van Tuan; Minister of Agriculture and Environment Trinh Viet Hung; Minister of Science and Technology Vu Hai Quan; Minister of Home Affairs Do Thanh Binh; Minister of Construction Tran Hong Minh; Minister of Culture, Sports and Tourism Lam Thi Phuong Thanh; Minister of Education and Training Hoang Minh Son; Minister of Industry and Trade Le Manh Hung; Minister of Justice Hoang Thanh Tung; Minister of Health Dao Hong Lan; Minister of Ethnic and Religious Affairs Nguyen Dinh Khang; Governor of the State Bank of Viet Nam Pham Duc An; Secretary of Hai Phong Municipal Party Committee Le Ngoc Chau; Secretary of Da Nang Municipal Party Committee Le Ngoc Quang; Secretary of Can Tho Municipal Party Committee Le Quang Tung; Secretary of Hue Municipal Party Committee Nguyen Dinh Trung; and Secretary of Dong Nai Municipal Party Committee Vu Hong Van./.
