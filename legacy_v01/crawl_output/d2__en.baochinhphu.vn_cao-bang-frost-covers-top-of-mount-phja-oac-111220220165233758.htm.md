# Cao Bang: Frost covers top of Mount Phja Oac 
##  VGP - Thick ice and frost have appeared on the peak of Mount Phja Oac in the northern mountainous province of Cao Bang since early February 20, as temperatures dropped to below zero degree Celsius. 
February 20, 2022 4:52 PM GMT+7 
[![Cao Bang: Frost covers top of Mount Phja Oac
 - Ảnh 1.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb1-16453504221001251144167.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb1-16453504221001251144167.jpg "At a height of 1,930m above sea level, Phja Oac is the roof of Cao Bang. Frost and ice are normal phenomena here in winter.")
At a height of 1,930m above sea level, Phja Oac is the roof of Cao Bang. Frost and ice are normal phenomena here in winter.
[![Cao Bang: Frost covers top of Mount Phja Oac
 - Ảnh 2.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb3-16453504221331810253879.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb3-16453504221331810253879.jpg "Also on February 20, the National Centre for Hydro-Meteorological Forecasting predicted the cold air will continue to affect other parts of the central and south central regions.")
Also on February 20, the National Centre for Hydro-Meteorological Forecasting predicted the cold air will continue to affect other parts of the central and south central regions.
[![Cao Bang: Frost covers top of Mount Phja Oac
 - Ảnh 3.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb4-16453504221821230625866.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb4-16453504221821230625866.jpg "From February 20 to February 21, the areas from Quang Binh to Thua Thien-Hue will see rain, showers and thunderstorms, with possible whirlwinds, lightning, hail, and strong winds.")
From February 20 to February 21, the areas from Quang Binh to Thua Thien-Hue will see rain, showers and thunderstorms, with possible whirlwinds, lightning, hail, and strong winds.
[![Cao Bang: Frost covers top of Mount Phja Oac
 - Ảnh 4.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb5-16453504222312116141118.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2022/2/20/cb5-16453504222312116141118.jpg "From February 20 to 22, in the North and the north central province of Thanh Hoa, the lowest temperatures are forecast at 8-11 degrees Celsius, even zero degree Celsius in the high mountains with a high possibility of frost and snowfall.")
From February 20 to 22, in the North and the north central province of Thanh Hoa, the lowest temperatures are forecast at 8-11 degrees Celsius, even zero degree Celsius in the high mountains with a high possibility of frost and snowfall.

Meanwhile, the temperatures of areas from Nghe An province to Thua Thien-Hue province in the central region will be 13-16 degrees Celsius at the lowest.
