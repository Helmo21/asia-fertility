# General Secretary To Lam attends victory parade in Astana
##  VGP - General Secretary To Lam observed the military parade marking the 80th anniversary of Victory in the Second World War in Astana, Kazakhstan on April 7, as part of his three-day State visit to Kazakhstan. 
Author [Kim Anh | ](javascript:; "Kim Anh")
May 07, 2025 12:51 PM GMT+7 
[![General Secretary To Lam attends victory parade in Astana    - Ảnh 1.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-le-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-174660714950114245252.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-le-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-174660714950114245252.jpg "President Qasym-Jomart Toqayev and General Secretary of the Communist Party of Viet Nam To Lam observe the event from the official tribune - Photo: VNA")
President Qasym-Jomart Toqayev and General Secretary of the Communist Party of Viet Nam To Lam observe the event from the official tribune - Photo: VNA
In his speech, Supreme Commander-in-Chief Qasym-Jomart Toqayev especially acknowledged the contribution of Kazakhstan's citizens to the defeat of fascism. Over 1.2 million people from the republic were drafted, half of whom gave their lives for freedom and future generations.
[![General Secretary To Lam attends victory parade in Astana    - Ảnh 2.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/photo2025-05-0712-05-47-1-1746607609073717054624.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/photo2025-05-0712-05-47-1-1746607609073717054624.jpg "This marks the first state visit to Kazakhstan by a key Vietnamese leader to Kazakhstan in 13 years and the first ever by a CPV General Secretary.")
This marks the first state visit to Kazakhstan by a key Vietnamese leader to Kazakhstan in 13 years and the first ever by a CPV General Secretary.
"We approached the 80th Victory anniversary with special attention. A decision has been made to name about 500 streets across the country after Kazakh soldiers," said Qasym-Jomart Toqayev.
[![General Secretary To Lam attends victory parade in Astana    - Ảnh 3.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-to-lam-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-3-1746607405383647988320.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-to-lam-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-3-1746607405383647988320.jpg "Over 4,000 servicemembers march across the square during the military parade in Astana, featuring more than 200 units of weaponry and equipment, including 66 aircraft and helicopters.")
Over 4,000 servicemembers march across the square during the military parade in Astana, featuring more than 200 units of weaponry and equipment, including 66 aircraft and helicopters.
"Our foreign policy is aimed at ensuring this goal. Kazakhs are a peaceful, yet courageous and resolute people. Strengthening the country’s defense capability is our top priority. We have taken a firm course toward modernizing the Armed Forces and enhancing the army’s potential," said Toqayev.
[![General Secretary To Lam attends victory parade in Astana    - Ảnh 4.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-to-lam-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-5-17466074977301208481394.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2025/5/7/0705-tong-bi-thu-to-lam-duyet-binh-kazakhstan-5-17466074977301208481394.jpg "Aircraft flying overhead include the A-400 military transport aircraft, the L-410 passenger plane, and the Be-300 patrol aircraft. Photo: VNA")
Aircraft flying overhead include the A-400 military transport aircraft, the L-410 passenger plane, and the Be-300 patrol aircraft. Photo: VNA
