# Photo: Second parade drill for 80th National Day
##  VGP - As many as 16,000 officers and soldiers from the Ministry of National Defence and the Ministry of Public Security on Tuesday joined the second joint rehearsal for the military parade and march celebrating the 80th anniversary of the August Revolution (August 19, 1945 – 2025) and National Day (September 2, 1945 – 2025).
