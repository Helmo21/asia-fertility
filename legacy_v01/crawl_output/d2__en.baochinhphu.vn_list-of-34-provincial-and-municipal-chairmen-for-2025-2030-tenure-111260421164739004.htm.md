# List of 34 provincial and municipal Chairmen for 2026-2031 tenure
##  VGP - The Viet Nam Government Portal (VGP) respectfully presents the list of 34 Chairmen of the municipal and provincial People’s Committees for the 2026-2031 term, updated as of April 21, 2026. 
Author [Kim Anh](javascript:; "Kim Anh") | 
April 22, 2026 8:19 AM GMT+7 
[![List of 34 provincial and municipal Chairmen for 2026-2031 tenure- Ảnh 1.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2026/4/21/drinking-water1776764586-1776764602779352538845.png)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2026/4/21/drinking-water1776764586-1776764602779352538845.png)
Chairmen of provincial/municipal People’s Committees are the heads of local executive authorities in Viet Nam, responsible for organizing and ensuring the effective implementation of the Constitution, laws, and policies of the State at the provincial, district, or communal level. 
  * #### [INFOGRAPHICS: List of 34 provincial and municipal Party Secretaries for 2025-2030 tenure](https://en.baochinhphu.vn/infographics-list-of-34-provincial-and-municipal-party-secretaries-for-2025-2030-tenure-111260417112738323.htm)
  * #### [Updating: Major socio-economic development targets of 34 provinces and cities](https://en.baochinhphu.vn/major-socio-economic-development-targets-of-34-provinces-and-cities-111250923143038381.htm)
  * #### [Names and administrative centers of 34 provinces and centrally-run cities specified](https://en.baochinhphu.vn/names-and-administrative-centers-of-34-provinces-and-centrally-run-cities-specified-111250415094350491.htm)


They oversee the formulation and execution of socio-economic development plans, state budget management, land administration, and public service delivery within their respective administrative boundaries. 
As the chief administrators, chairmen assign tasks to vice chairmen and relevant departments, coordinate inter-agency work, and ensure administrative efficiency and discipline. They also play a key role in maintaining national defense, security, and social order in their locality, while addressing citizens’ complaints and petitions, promoting transparency, and being accountable to both higher-level authorities and the local People’s Council./.
