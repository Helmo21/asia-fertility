# Gov't announces list of 142 conditional business lines
##  VGP - The Government has issued Resolution No. 66.17/2026/NQ-CP approving the list of 142 conditional business lines, applicable from July 1, 2026. 
[Huong Giang](javascript:; "Huong Giang")
May 20, 2026 3:06 PM GMT+7
