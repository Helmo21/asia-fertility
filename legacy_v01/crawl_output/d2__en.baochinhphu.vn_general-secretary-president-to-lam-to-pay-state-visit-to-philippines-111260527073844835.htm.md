# General Secretary, President To Lam to pay State visit to Philippines
##  VGP – General Secretary and President To Lam is set to pay a State visit to the Philippines from May 31 to June 1, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on Wednesday. 
[Huong Giang](javascript:; "Huong Giang")
May 27, 2026 7:38 AM GMT+7 
[![General Secretary, President To Lam to pay State visit to Philippines- Ảnh 1.](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2026/5/27/tong-bi-thu-to-lam-17614761602661776679649-1775703758099971702570-1-17796351549521080359784-17798418972821472604512.jpg)](https://bcp.cdnchinhphu.vn/344443456812359680/2026/5/27/tong-bi-thu-to-lam-17614761602661776679649-1775703758099971702570-1-17796351549521080359784-17798418972821472604512.jpg "General Secretary and President To Lam")
[ ](https://en.baochinhphu.vn/general-secretary-president-to-lam-to-pay-state-visit-to-philippines-111260527073844835.htm&quote=General%20Secretary%20and%20President%20To%20Lam "Share Facebook") [ ](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=General%20Secretary%20and%20President%20To%20Lam%20https://en.baochinhphu.vn/general-secretary-president-to-lam-to-pay-state-visit-to-philippines-111260527073844835.htm "Share Twitter")
General Secretary and President To Lam
The visit will be made at the invitation of President of the Philippines Ferdinand Romualdez Marcos Jr.
Viet Nam and the Philippines set up diplomatic relations on July 12, 1976, and officially signed an agreement to upgrade their relationship to a strategic partnership on November 17, 2015./.
