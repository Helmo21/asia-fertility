# Prime Minister hosts welcome ceremony for Malaysian counterpart
##  VGP - Prime Minister Pham Minh Chinh hosted an official welcome ceremony for Malaysian Prime Minister Dato' Sri Ismail Sabri bin Yaakob at the Presidential Palace in Ha Noi on Monday morning. 
Author [Huong Giang | ](javascript:; "Huong Giang")
March 21, 2022 11:48 AM GMT+7
