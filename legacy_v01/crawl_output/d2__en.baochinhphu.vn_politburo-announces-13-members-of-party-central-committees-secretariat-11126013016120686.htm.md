# Politburo announces 13 members of Party Central Committee's Secretariat
##  VGP – General Secretary To Lam on Friday signed a decision assigning 10 Politburo members to join the Party Central Committee's Secretariat. 
[Huong Giang](javascript:; "Huong Giang")
January 30, 2026 4:11 PM GMT+7 
With this decision, the Secretariat shall comprise 13 members, including 10 Politburo members and three Secretaries.
1. Comrade To Lam, General Secretary of the 14th Central Committee of the Communist Party of Viet Nam, Secretary of the Central Military Commission.
2. Comrade Tran Cam Tu, Politburo member, Standing member of the Party Central Committee's Secretariat.
3. Comrade Le Minh Hung, Politburo member, Chairman of the Central Inspection Commission.
4. Comrade Bui Thi Minh Hoai, Politburo member, President of the Viet Nam Fatherland Front Central Committee.
5. Comrade Nguyen Trong Nghia, Politburo member, Chairman of the General Department of Politics of the Viet Nam People's Army.
6. Comrade Trinh Van Quyet, Politburo member, Chairman of the Central Commission for Education, Communication and Mass Mobilization.
7. Comrade Le Minh Tri, Politburo member, Chairman of the Central Commission for Internal Affairs.
8. Comrade Pham Gia Tuc, Politburo member, Chairman of the Party Central Committee's Office.
9. Comrade Tran Sy Thanh, Politburo member, Chairman of the Central Inspection Commission.
10. Comrade Nguyen Thanh Nghi, Politburo member, Chairman of the Central Policy and Strategy Commission.
11. Comrade Vo Thi Anh Xuan, member of the Party Central Committee, Vice State President.
12. Comrade Pham Thi Thanh Tra member of the Party Central Committee, Deputy Prime Minister.
13. Comrade Nguyen Van Quang, member of the Party Central Committee, Chief Judge of the Supreme People's Court.
